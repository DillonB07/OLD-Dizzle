Info - Dizzle

Welcome to the information page for Dizzle! Everything you need to know to use Dizzle is here!

Question Types

  • Memory

    Example: "What Queen of England died in 1901?"
    The answer to this question is Queen Victoria. Unless otherwise stated you need to list the full name for real people or places.

  • Multi-choice

    Example: "What was the first opera performed at the Sydney Opera House? - War and Peace, Madame Butterfly or Porgy and the Bess?"
    You will be given three options. To get the answer correct, you must type in the answer as it is given to you in the question. So, no typos, no missing words - War & Peace would not be accepted, but War and Peace would be -, but you can use uppercasem lowercase or a mixture.

  • True or False

    Example: "True or False: The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe is the first book in the Chronicles of Narnia."
    For this question, you just need to enter True or False.

  • Fill in the blank

    Example: "Fill in the blank: In the 1980s, Nina sang about 99 _____ balloons." This is simple. Just fill in the missing word. The number of underscores does not tell you the length of the word.
